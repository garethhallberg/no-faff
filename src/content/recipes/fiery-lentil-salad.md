Quick and easy salad dish. Serve it with a couple of other simple sides and save what's left for lunch tomorrow.

## Ingredients

- 440g can lentils (or dried if you have the time).
- 1-2 green finger chillies
- 1 teaspoon red wine vinegar
- 1 glug of extra virgin olive oil
- Salt and pepper

## Method

### 1. Lentils

Cook the lentils if they are dried. Otherwise open the can and drain.

### 2. Chillie

Chop it/them up into tiny pieces. Go for two if like the heat.

### 3. Mix it all up

Add the lentils to a bowl. Add the chillies. Pour your glug of extra virgin olive oil. Add the teaspoon of red wine vinegar. Season with salt and pepper and taste. If you think it needs more vinegar go ahead and add a drop more.

### 4. Wait

Leave for at least ten minutes, longer of possible. Don't skip this step.

## Why this works

Lentils are packed with protein and fibre. They have an earthiness which the chilli and vinegar work well with.

## Make it immense

- Capers. Always capers.
- A handful of cornichons, sliced.

## No-faff swaps

- No green chilli? Try a red one or even use chilli flakes.
- No red wine vinegar? White wine vinegar. Cider vinegar. Balsamic vinegar. Lemon juice at a pinch.
